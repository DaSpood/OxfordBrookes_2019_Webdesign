Our products

Our extensive selection of beautifully handmade hand-painted Moroccan ceramics includes: ceramic bowls with silver or copper edge of different sizes, fruit bowls, ceramic eggcups, safi plates and ceramic jewellery boxes. Our safi ceramics are truly stunning pieces of interior decoration that will look amazing for serving food with style and as a centre piece on your table.
